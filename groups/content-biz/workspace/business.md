## Cross-Cutting Learnings

### Creator Quality Signals
- Mid-tier creators (10K-100K) often have better engagement rates than mega-creators
- Humor + technical depth is a rare and valuable combination (e.g., Kai Lentit)
- Consistency matters: creators who post 2x/month minimum maintain audience trust
- Cross-platform presence (newsletter + social) amplifies sponsor value

### Platform Patterns
- Newsletters: Fastest to activate, best attribution via shortlinks, 2-4 week lead times
- YouTube: Longer lead time (1-2 months), attribution harder, but high brand awareness
- LinkedIn: Strong for B2B/technical audience, especially AI/ML engineers
- X/Twitter: Brand awareness play, good for reposting/amplification, weak attribution
- Short-form video (TikTok/Reels): Growing dev content space, quick turnaround

### Sponsor Patterns
- Series B+ companies more likely to have creator budgets ($50k+/mo)
- Dev tools companies need creators with GitHub presence
- Brand sponsors want category exclusivity (single sponsor per series/quarter)
- Long-term series (6+ weeks) outperform one-off placements
- "How did you hear about us?" is critical for attribution when promo codes don't apply

### Pricing Benchmarks
- Newsletter dedicated post: $10-15k (depending on audience size)
- Newsletter banner: $2-5k
- YouTube integration: Varies widely, $5-25k depending on creator size
- LinkedIn posts: $2k per post
- Program management retainer: $10-15k/mo (on top of creator spend)
