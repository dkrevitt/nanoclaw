## Insights

### Why it's hard to find small OpenClaw creators:
1. **OpenClaw is still relatively new** - early adopters tend to be larger channels
2. **Tutorial content** surfaces larger creators (YouTube algorithm favors them)
3. **View counts don't always indicate channel size** - viral videos can inflate numbers

### Better search strategies:
1. Sort by "Upload date" instead of "Relevance" to find newer creators
2. Search for specific OpenClaw features/integrations (niche topics)
3. Look for "vs" comparisons or "alternatives to" content
4. Search for local language content (non-English markets)
