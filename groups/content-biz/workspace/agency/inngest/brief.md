# Inngest - Agency Client

**Website:** [inngest.com](https://inngest.com)
**Stage:** Series B (estimated)
**Status:** Late stage negotiations

## Company Overview

Inngest provides durable execution infrastructure - background jobs, workflows, and queuing without the infrastructure headache. Key product for both traditional backend developers and AI engineers building agents.

## Program Goals

- Drive offsite awareness across creator channels (newsletters + social)
- Target: $50k/mo spend at ~$100 CPM
- Split budget across two personas

## Target Personas

| Persona | Positioning | Target Channels |
|---------|-------------|-----------------|
| **Backend Engineers** | "Background jobs, workflows, queuing — without the infrastructure headache" | Cooper Press (Node Weekly, JavaScript Weekly), Bytes.dev, Hacker Newsletter, backend/infra YouTube |
| **AI Engineers** | "Building AI agents? You need durable execution in your stack" | TLDR AI, AI-specific Substacks, AI coding channels, agent-building tutorials |

## Platform Strategy

| Platform | Notes |
|----------|-------|
| Newsletter | Easiest starting point. Well-read, durable, affordable. Good attribution via shortlinks. |
| YouTube | Mix of ad placements (awareness) + deeper partnerships. Attribution harder - use shortlinks + promo codes + "how did you hear" |
| X/Twitter | Real-time visibility, reposting. Lower cost, high volume. Essentially a RT ring. |
| Instagram/TikTok | Short-form vertical. Open to testing, especially for AI persona. |

## Measurement

- Primary: Views at ~$100 CPM
- Secondary: Activated signups (promo codes, shortlinks, "how did you hear")

## Process

1. Source creators across both personas + platforms
2. Run outreach, negotiate, book initial packages
3. Start with newsletters (fastest) + YouTube (longer lead), layer on short-form + X
4. Weekly briefing doc to creator roster with topic ideas
5. Measure and iterate - shift budget to what works

## Pricing

- Management fee: $10k/mo retainer
- Creator spend: $50k/mo target when ramped
- Term: 3-month initial (3-month renewals)

## Key Risks

1. AI persona is unproven - Inngest not well-known in AI agent context yet
2. Ramp time - Month 1 lighter spend
3. Attribution gaps - YouTube/short-form harder to track
4. Free tier impact - promo codes less useful when free tier is generous
