## Company Overview

Tavily provides AI search/research API - used by developers building AI applications with search capabilities and teams building internal research tools.

## Current State (as of January 2026)

- Primary organic channel: CEO's LinkedIn
- Company channels have limited engagement
- X/Twitter presence minimal (competitors like Firecrawl more active)
- Product team ships fast (2-day lead times)
- Internal culture not oriented toward content creation
- Upcoming activations: 4 trade shows in spring, subway ads in February

## Target Segments

| Segment | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **AI Applications** | Developers building AI products with search/research capabilities |
| **Internal Automation** | Teams building internal chatbots, research tools for specific verticals (finance/legal/HR) |

## Proposed Program

### Phase 1: Market Mapping ($15k, 3 weeks)

Discovery project to map the technical creator opportunity.

**Week 1:** Personas + topic clusters for each segment
**Week 2:** Creator database (50+ creators across platforms/segments)
**Week 3:** Budget + ROI modeling with recommendations

**Platforms to map:**
- Long-form video (YouTube)
- Text-based social (X, LinkedIn)
- Short-form vertical video (TikTok, Instagram Reels, YT Shorts)
- Newsletter

### Phase 2: Creator Program Management ($15k/mo)

Contingent on Phase 1 findings.

- Sourcing + outreach (direct relationships, no intermediaries)
- Content planning + publishing
- Creator newsletter with weekly/less frequent digest
- Payment management

**Requirements:** $50k/mo minimum creator spend, 6-month commitment

## Constraints

1. Need clarity on segments before scaling spend
2. Creators must match fast shipping cadence (2-day product lead times)
3. Prioritize shorter turnaround formats (vertical video, text+image)
4. Prioritize direct relationships over marketplaces

## Key Risks

1. Pace matching - fast shipping may limit long-form content options
2. Competitive landscape - Firecrawl already active on X
