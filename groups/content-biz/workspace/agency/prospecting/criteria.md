# Agency Prospecting Criteria

Criteria for finding companies to pitch agency services.

## Pre-Filters

minFunding: Series Seed
maxFunding: Series B (larger companies have internal teams)
requireB2B: true
requireTechAdjacent: true

## must_have

- **Technical product**: Developer tools or adjacent technical/semi-technical B2B products
- **Technical audience**: Targeting sophisticated/technical decision makers
- **Marketing budget**: Able to commit $50k+/mo in creator spend
- **Marketing leader**: Head of Marketing or DevRel who can champion the program
- **Growth stage**: Series Seed - Series B (sweet spot for needing external help)

## nice_to_have

- Already doing some creator marketing (signals they value channel)
- Fast shipping cadence (more content opportunities)
- Active competitor creator programs (validates the space)
- Clear personas and positioning (easier to execute)
- Founder/team active on social (amplification potential)

## exclude_if

- Enterprise sales motion only (too slow, creator marketing less relevant)
- Has internal creator program team (don't need us)
- Budget under $50k/mo (not worth program overhead)
- No clear technical angle (can't find right creators)
- Series C+ (usually have internal resources)

## Signals to Watch For

### Positive Signals
- Recent funding announcement (budget unlocked)
- Hiring for marketing/DevRel (building team)
- Competitor creator partnerships visible
- Active on Product Hunt, Hacker News (developer-focused)
- Open source component (creator-friendly)

### Negative Signals
- Enterprise-only pricing (wrong sales motion)
- No developer documentation (not dev-focused)
- Heavy sales-led GTM (creators less relevant)
- Mature company with established brand (less need)

## Discovery Approach

1. **Research**: Company, product, current marketing, competitors' creator programs
2. **Find buyer**: Head of Marketing, VP Marketing, or DevRel lead
3. **Hook**: Reference their space, competitors' creator activity, or recent news
4. **Offer**: Start with Phase 1 mapping if they need clarity, or full program if ready
