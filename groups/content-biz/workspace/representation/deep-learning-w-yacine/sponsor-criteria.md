# Sponsor Criteria: Deep Learning with Yacine

## Audience Context

Small but highly valuable audience: ML researchers, PhD students, senior engineers at frontier AI labs. This is about credibility and recruiting, not mass reach.

## Pre-Filters

requireAIResearch: true (must be actively publishing AI research)
requireTechnicalDepth: true

## must_have

- **AI research focus**: Organization must be actively publishing AI research
- **Research credibility**: Real papers, not just marketing
- **Recruiting interest**: Wants to reach ML researchers and PhD students
- **Quarterly commitment**: Willing to commit to quarter-long sponsorship

## nice_to_have

- Published papers that Yacine can walk through on stream
- Interesting technical approach worth explaining
- Open-source research or tools
- Research team willing to appear as guests

## exclude_if

- No published research (marketing-only AI companies)
- Consumer products
- Looking for mass reach (not the right channel)
- Short-term / one-off placements

## Ideal Sponsor Categories

### Tier 1 (Perfect Fit)
- **Frontier AI labs**: OpenAI, Anthropic, DeepMind, Meta AI
- **Research-focused startups**: Companies publishing papers
- **GPU/compute providers**: NVIDIA, AMD, cloud providers with ML focus

### Tier 2 (Good Fit)
- **ML infrastructure**: Tools used in research workflows
- **Model training/serving**: Platforms for researchers
- **Academic publishers/platforms**: Research-adjacent

### Tier 3 (Possible Fit)
- **Developer tools with research use cases**
- **Companies wanting employer brand in AI research**

## Sponsorship Structure

Quarterly partnerships with category exclusivity:

| Element | Deliverables |
|---------|--------------|
| Stream mentions | Brief acknowledgment at start of each weekly stream |
| Clip sponsor cards | Visual sponsor card in each clip (3-4 per week) |
| Social posts | X + LinkedIn posts covering streams |
| Research highlight | Minimum 1 stream/quarter dedicated to sponsor's paper |

## Pricing

Quarterly sponsorship pricing TBD - position based on value of audience (recruiting top ML talent) rather than raw reach.

## Outreach Approach

This sponsorship is a brand/employer brand investment. Pitch should emphasize:
1. Audience quality (PhD students, ML researchers)
2. Research credibility association
3. Recruiting reach (exact talent pool competing labs recruit from)
4. Category exclusivity value
