## What It Is

Decoding AI teaches AI engineers how to build production-grade AI systems. "No bedtime stories" - practical, hands-on engineering content focused on shipping AI systems that work in the real world.

**Tagline:** "Real-world guides taking you from PoC purgatory to shipping AI products that work."
