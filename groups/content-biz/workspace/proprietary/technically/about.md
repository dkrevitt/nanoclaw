# Technically

**Website:** [technically.dev](https://technically.dev) | [Newsletter](https://read.technically.dev)
**Founded:** 2019 by Justin Gage
**Subscribers:** ~72,000 (growing ~25% YoY)
**Open Rate:** 35-40%
**CTR:** ~0.5% on sponsored posts

## What It Is

Technically is a knowledge base for learning about software concepts. Known for "Explain Like I'm 5" style posts on technical topics like APIs, cloud computing, databases, data warehouses, ETL, and AI.

## Content Types

1. **Tech explainers** - Simple explanations of technical concepts (APIs, cloud, databases)
2. **Company deep dives** - "What does [Company] do?" breakdowns
3. **Product category explainers** - How specific product types work
4. **AI series** - "AI: It's Not That Complicated" content series

## Audience

**Historical:** Software engineers, backend/infrastructure engineers, technical practitioners

**Shifting to:** Startup operators - founders, product people, finance/ops, sales + marketing leaders who need to understand tech but aren't engineers themselves. People at tech companies, banks, financial services, healthcare who interact with software but lack deep technical expertise.
