# Sponsor Criteria: Technically

## Strategic Shift

**Historical sponsors:** Dev tools companies (MongoDB, Vercel, PostHog, etc.)

**New target:** Companies marketing to startup operators - not just developers, but founders, product people, finance/ops, sales + marketing leaders.

## Pre-Filters

minFunding: Series A (prefer Series B+)
requireB2B: true
requireTechAdjacent: true

## must_have

- **Startup operator audience**: Product serves founders, product managers, finance/ops, or GTM leaders (not just developers)
- **Explainable product**: Can be broken down into "What does X do?" or "How does X work?" format
- **Budget for content**: $15k+ for dedicated posts, $50k+ for series
- **Willing to collaborate**: Available for interviews, review cycles, and content input

## nice_to_have

- Has existing content marketing motion (signals they value content)
- Growing category or emerging space (more interesting to readers)
- Story angle beyond product (company origin, market insight, trend)
- Cross-platform presence (can amplify on their own channels)
- Customer stories available (for "how things get built" deep dives)

## exclude_if

- Pure dev tools with no operator angle (unless willing to reframe)
- Consumer products
- No budget for content sponsorship
- Requires heavy approval process that slows production

## Ideal Sponsor Profiles

### Startup Infrastructure
- Finance/accounting tools (Ramp, Brex, Mercury competitors)
- HR/people ops (Rippling, Gusto competitors)
- Legal/compliance (Vanta competitors)
- Sales/GTM tools (Gong, Outreach competitors)

### Product/Growth Tools
- Product analytics (PostHog, Amplitude, Mixpanel)
- A/B testing / experimentation
- Customer feedback / research tools
- Growth engineering tools

### AI for Operators
- AI writing/content tools
- AI for sales/marketing
- AI for finance/ops
- AI assistants for non-technical users
